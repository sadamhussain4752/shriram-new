ABOUT BUILDER

Shriram Properties, real estate developers and builders, is committed to providing quality and affordable housing and commercial properties in Bangalore, Chennai, Vizag and Kolkata. For over a decade now, Shriram Properties has laid emphasis on addressing the lifestyle needs of the customer, building quality and affordable apartments that provide optimal value for money. With an excellent track record of completing major projects in Tier 1 & Tier 2 cities, Shriram Properties envisages establishing a pan-India presence with a portfolio of projects, including residential apartments, modern integrated townships, commercial, retail, hospitality and SEZs.
